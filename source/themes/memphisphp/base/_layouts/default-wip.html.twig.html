Are you a PHP Developer in Memphis? Are you just curious about PHP? Come meet other MemphisPHP developers at our monthly meet ups.

We meet on the 4th Tuesday of each month.

MemphisPHP is a community of PHP developers and open source enthusiasts in the Memphis, TN area founded May 23, 2010 by Jeremy Kendall.

The current coorganizers are David Haskins and Joe Ferguson
